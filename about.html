About Us

Puppy Cozy is the home of premium knitwear sweaters that dogs genuinely love.

We design and produce high-quality, comfortable, and simple dog sweaters. Every piece is meticulously crafted using our own production of 100% premium cotton fabric.

Here's something special we've noticed: dogs develop a real connection with our sweaters and often insist on keeping them on! We believe it has everything to do with the softness of our natural fabric and the way it gently wraps around them, providing comfort that makes them feel like you're holding them—even when you aren't.

Our mission is simple: to keep dogs warm, comfortable, and happy while giving pet parents peace of mind knowing their furry friends are wearing safe, natural materials.

Follow us on social media to join the pack and see happy pups in their cozy sweaters!

— The Puppy Cozy Team
