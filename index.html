Do Dogs Feel Cold?

While all dogs can feel the cold, puppies, smaller breeds, short-haired dogs, and senior dogs are especially vulnerable and feel it more sharply.

It's important to keep them warm when temperatures drop, especially during walks when they're outside in the cold. A good sweater can make all the difference in keeping your furry friend comfortable and happy.

What Makes Our Sweaters Special?

Natural Materials

Many dogs suffer from allergies that cause itching and sneezing. Synthetic fibers often contain chemicals used during manufacturing that can trigger allergic reactions and harbor bacteria, mold, and dust mites.

That's why our sweaters are made from 100% natural cotton that's hypoallergenic, gentle on sensitive skin, and safe for even the most allergy-prone pups.

Stretchy & Warm

Using special knitting techniques, we've added incredible elasticity to natural cotton fabric without any synthetic materials.

Our stretchy fabric fits small to medium-sized dogs perfectly, adapting to different body shapes and wrapping around your pet like a second skin for maximum comfort.

The tight rib stitching traps heat in its layers, keeping your dog warm and cozy even on the coldest days.

Easy to Care For

Our sweaters are machine-washable, making them easy to keep clean and fresh.

The durable, high-tensile-strength fabric maintains vibrant colors wash after wash, so your dog always looks their best.

Certified Safety

STANDARD 100 by OEKO-TEX® is one of the world's most recognized labels for textiles tested for harmful substances. It represents customer confidence and exceptional product safety.

Our fabric materials are tested by independent institutes and certified to meet the same strict requirements as baby clothing, ensuring they're completely safe for your beloved pet.
